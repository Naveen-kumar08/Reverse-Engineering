# Bicycle Reverse Engineering Project

This project involves **disassembling and assembling a bicycle** to study its components and understand its design and functionality. It includes **technical sketches, CAD drawings, and analysis** of bicycle parts to learn mechanical design principles.

---

## Activities Performed

- **Disassembly & Assembly** of bicycle components  
- **Freehand Sketching** of parts and assemblies  
- **AutoCAD Drawings** for precise technical representation  
- **Component Analysis** for design improvements  

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Understanding of mechanical parts and their functions  
- Practical skills in sketching and CAD design  
- Knowledge of assembly techniques and mechanical design  
- Ability to suggest improvements for component efficiency
